About Pocket Pulse

Pocket Pulse is your smart expense tracker — a web application that makes managing money simple. It records all your transactions and presents them as clear, transparent notes.

Our goal is to help people handle their finances without hassle. This version demonstrates the core functionality, while we’re also working on Pocket Pulse 2.0 with more predictive features powered by real data.
